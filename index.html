Welcome! I am a postdoctoral researcher in Economics at McGill University.
My research applies Microeconomics and Industrial Organization to tackle questions in Public Policy and Health.

You can find my CV here.

I am interested in studying agents' behaviors and how they can inform public policies. My main line of work focuses on the implications of consumers preferences for cannabis with regards to legalization and market regulation. Since joining McGill, I have also embarked on investigating the economics of cancer; in particular how cancer diagnosis and care inflence households' labor outcomes.
